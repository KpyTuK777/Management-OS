## Definition

The **Operational Organization Model** is a governed representation of how the
organization can participate in operational work. It extends hierarchy and
reporting relationships with relevant operating characteristics so an Investigation
can plan Evidence acquisition and action realistically.

Eligible context may include:

- teams, responsibilities, decision rights, reporting and escalation relationships;
- working hours, department availability, interview windows, and reporting cadence;
- declared communication preferences and expected response ranges;
- systems used, CRM adoption, ERP availability, and digital maturity at an
  appropriately governed organizational level;
- operational constraints, recurring bottlenecks, handoffs, travel requirements,
  Evidence availability, and department-specific operating notes.

Its purpose is Investigation planning, not organization-chart administration,
employee scoring, surveillance, or automatic judgment about people.

## Authority and relationship boundaries

The model is a supporting capability, not part of the Investigation aggregate and
not Organizational Knowledge or the Personal Operational Model. Source systems
retain authority. An Investigation references authorized current context and may
freeze a decision-relevant snapshot without rewriting the source.

Every material item preserves source, scope, authorization, sensitivity, freshness,
and correction path. Declared information is distinguished from observed behavior
and inferred characteristics. Individual-level behavioral inference, hidden
performance profiling, or expansion of access is prohibited without separately
approved architecture and authority.

AI may identify missing, stale, or conflicting organizational context and propose
a correction or collection action. It may not silently create, infer, approve, or
change durable organization facts.

## Investigation use

The model may inform feasibility, sequencing, expected delay, communication
channel, Evidence accessibility, and scheduling proposals. It never establishes a
Cause, overrides current Evidence, authorizes contact, reserves time, assigns work,
or determines a decision. Degraded operation exposes uncertainty and allows manual
planning without pretending missing context is known.
